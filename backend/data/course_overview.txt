Welcome to the Advanced Machine Learning Course!

This comprehensive course covers the fundamentals and advanced topics in machine learning, including:
- Supervised Learning (Classification and Regression)
- Unsupervised Learning (Clustering and Dimensionality Reduction)
- Deep Learning and Neural Networks
- Natural Language Processing
- Computer Vision
- Reinforcement Learning

Duration: 12 weeks
Format: Online with live sessions twice a week
Prerequisites: Basic Python programming and linear algebra
Instructor: Dr. Sarah Johnson

Course Structure:
- Week 1-3: Foundations of Machine Learning
- Week 4-6: Deep Learning Fundamentals
- Week 7-9: Advanced Topics (NLP, CV, RL)
- Week 10-12: Capstone Project

Students will work on hands-on projects and assignments throughout the course.
