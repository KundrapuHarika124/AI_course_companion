Course Certification Requirements

To earn the official course certificate, students must meet the following criteria:

1. Minimum Overall Grade: 70% or higher
   - This is calculated based on all grading components
   - See grading policy for breakdown

2. Assignment Completion: At least 8 out of 10 assignments must be submitted
   - Quality matters: assignments must score at least 50%

3. Capstone Project: Must be completed and presented
   - Minimum passing score: 60%
   - All team members must contribute

4. Attendance: Minimum 75% attendance in live sessions
   - 18 out of 24 sessions required

Certificate Details:
- Digital certificate issued within 2 weeks after course completion
- Includes course title, completion date, and final grade
- Signed by instructor and program director
- Verifiable through unique certificate ID
- Can be shared on LinkedIn and other professional platforms

Honor Code:
All work must be original. Plagiarism or cheating will result in:
- Zero on the assignment/exam
- Possible course failure
- No certificate issued

Certificate Calculation Example:
If a student scores:
- Assignments: 85% (0.85 × 40 = 34 points)
- Midterm: 78% (0.78 × 20 = 15.6 points)
- Capstone: 82% (0.82 × 30 = 24.6 points)
- Participation: 90% (0.90 × 10 = 9 points)
Total: 34 + 15.6 + 24.6 + 9 = 83.2% → Certificate Awarded ✓
