Grading Policy

Your final grade is calculated based on the following components:

1. Weekly Assignments (40%)
   - 10 assignments total
   - Each assignment worth 4%
   - Must be submitted by Sunday 11:59 PM
   - Late submissions: 10% penalty per day

2. Midterm Exam (20%)
   - Covers weeks 1-6
   - Multiple choice and coding problems
   - Scheduled in Week 7

3. Capstone Project (30%)
   - Team project (3-4 members)
   - Project proposal: 5%
   - Implementation: 15%
   - Final presentation: 10%

4. Class Participation (10%)
   - Active participation in live sessions
   - Forum discussions
   - Peer review of projects

Grading Scale:
- A: 90-100%
- B: 80-89%
- C: 70-79%
- D: 60-69%
- F: Below 60%

Note: A minimum of 70% is required to earn the course certificate.
